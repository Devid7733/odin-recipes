Nom Banh Chok

Description

Nom Banh Chok is a traditional Cambodian rice noodle dish served with a green fish curry gravy and fresh vegetables.

Ingredients

  • rice noodles
  • fish (boiled and flaked)
  • lemongrass, turmeric, galangal (for curry paste)
  • coconut milk
  • fresh herbs and vegetables (banana flower, cucumber, mint)

Steps

  1. Prepare curry paste with lemongrass, turmeric, and galangal.
  2. Cook the paste with coconut milk and add flaked fish.
  3. Boil rice noodles until soft and drain.
  4. Pour curry over noodles and top with sliced vegetables and herbs.
