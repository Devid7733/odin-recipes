Beef Lok Lak

Description

Beef Lok Lak is a stir-fried marinated beef dish served with rice, salad, and lime-pepper sauce.

Ingredients

  • beef steak (thinly sliced)
  • soy sauce
  • oyster sauce
  • black pepper
  • lettuce and tomatoes
  • lime and ground pepper for dipping sauce

Steps

  1. Marinate the beef in soy sauce, oyster sauce, and black pepper.
  2. Heat a pan and stir-fry the beef until cooked through.
  3. Arrange lettuce and sliced tomatoes on a plate.
  4. Place beef on top and serve with rice and lime-pepper dipping sauce.
